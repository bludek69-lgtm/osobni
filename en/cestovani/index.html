Travel

Across Europe — behind the wheel and for fun

For years I've driven a bus across Europe — for work and for pleasure. Italy, Croatia, Nordic tours, European tours for foreign clients. Some of it is in the gallery below.

What I love

Italy is closest to my heart — I lived part of my life in Tuscany, in the town of Lerici. I come back both for work and personally. The landscape, the food, the people. There's a calm I don't feel elsewhere.

Outside Italy I've seen Scandinavia, the Baltics, the British Isles, France, Germany and the whole Balkans from the driver's seat. Some were guided tours with clients, some were my own trips. Travel for me isn't continuous holidays — it's a long-term work and life experience.
