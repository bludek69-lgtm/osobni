Relation to Italy

I spent part of my life in Tuscany, in the seaside town of Lerici. Italy is not just a destination for me — it's a place where I lived everyday life, where I learned to understand the local culture, food and people.

I keep coming back, both for work (as a driver on tours) and personally. I love its landscape, cities, atmosphere and way of life. I love places that have a story, calm and distinct character — and Italy, especially Tuscany, has countless such places.
