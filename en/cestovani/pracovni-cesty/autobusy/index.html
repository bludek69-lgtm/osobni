Buses I drove

Buses are more than just equipment to a driver. Each vehicle has a slightly different character — handles differently on long routes, in the mountains, in city traffic. Here are the buses I met during my career or drove on European trips.

📌 Disclaimer: This gallery isn't a carrier catalog. It's a personal travel archive. Bus models are listed only where identifiable from the photo without speculation. Sensitive work details and license plates have been removed from photos or not published.

🔧 Identified models

  • Setra S 517 HD — German-made coach, common in Vega Tour fleet. 2-axle, high-decker, popular for European tours.
  • Setra S 415 HD — earlier generation, also common in tour operators.
  • Bova Magiq / Futura — Dutch coaches, occasional encounters at international parking lots.
  • MAN Lion's Coach — German chassis, popular in newer fleets.
  • BusLine fleet — mix of Iveco, Mercedes-Benz, SOR for regional/regular service.
