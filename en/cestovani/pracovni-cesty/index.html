Travel from the driver's perspective

Driver's work trips

Driver work has taken me to many places in Europe over the years. Not just driving, but discovering cities, countries and people. Behind the wheel of a bus I reached resort trips, regular trips to Croatia and Italy, and incoming European tours for foreign clients.

This part of the site captures work travel through a driver's eyes — buses, routes, stops, cities and places I had the chance to know through my job.

📌 Note: This section is not advertising for any carrier. It's a personal travel archive. Sensitive work details (client names, contractual details, plate numbers) have been removed from photos or not published. Detailed itineraries and internal information are not here.
