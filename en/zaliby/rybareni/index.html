Hobbies

🎣 Fishing

By the water, a man clears his head. For a moment deadlines, phones and traffic disappear — only water, line and waiting remain. That's how I picture it.

What I love about it

Fishing for me isn't about the catch, it's about being by the water. Early morning, mist on the surface, silence broken only by a splash or the bite alarm — that's a kind of rest I don't get behind the wheel.

Seasonally a few trips a year, usually with a friend or alone. Classic locations like Doksy and similar spots in northern Bohemia.
