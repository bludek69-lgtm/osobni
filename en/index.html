About me

I've been behind the wheel since 1997. Part of my life I spent in the Tuscan seaside town of Lerici. In between I ran a computer business and an e-shop. Today I drive for a regional carrier and spend my free time on personal projects.

Italy has a special place in my heart — I lived there, work there, and keep coming back. The food, the regions, the rhythm of daily life. Most of the travel content on this site reflects that.

What I'm into

  • 🇮🇹 Italy and Italian cuisine — I cook at home, return there for work and pleasure.
  • 🏠 Smart home — a system that handles heating, lights, and the morning rhythm.
  • 💰 Investing — gold, stocks, ETFs, crypto. Patience > speculation.
  • 🤖 AI and modern tech — I work with AI agents, do a lot myself.
  • 🎣 Fishing — being by the water, early morning, mist on the surface.
