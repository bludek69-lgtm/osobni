🎾TenisPark

One app for a whole small tennis club: home budget, court bookings and a snack kiosk. Instead of three notebooks and a calculator — everything in one place, on your PC, no internet needed. No cloud, no bank connection, no online payments.

React TypeScript SQLite Tauri (Windows)

What it does

Why it exists: anyone who looks after a few tennis courts with a kiosk knows it: bookings in one notebook, "on the tab" (who drank what and hasn't paid) in another, counting the till on a calculator in the evening and personal money in a third one. TenisPark joins it all into one app that absolutely anyone can use — every page has an expandable "how to" help.

Tennis courts: bookings take a few clicks — court, from–to, name. The app won't allow two people on the same court at the same time, the price is pre-filled from the price list (different for weekdays and weekends), and after payment you click "Paid". Plus maintenance records (lining, watering) and occupancy and revenue reports per day and month.

Kiosk: sales with a stock that watches itself — you can't sell more than is in stock, and the app warns when something is running low. A special feature is the debtors book: whoever doesn't pay right away gets their consumption written "on the tab" with their name, and whoever doesn't pay within 3 days lights up red. In the evening you run the daily closing — the app compares how much should be in the till with what's really there. When prices change, the old price stays visible crossed out next to the new one.

Home budget: personal income and expenses like receipts, categories (envelopes), monthly limits "how much I want to spend on food", plus a year-round planning section for seasonal work — how much to put aside in summer so winter works out.

Everything local and safe: data lives in an SQLite database on your PC only, backs up with one click to a file with a checksum, and a restore always shows a preview first. The app has a light and dark look in clay-court colours.

Key features

  • Collision-free court bookings — the app simply won't allow a double booking of the same court; weekday/weekend price list, states Booked / Paid / Cancelled / No-show.
  • Kiosk with stock — every sale lowers the stock, minimum levels are watched, you can't sell more than is in stock. The daily closing compares the till with sales.
  • Debtors book — consumption "on the tab" with a name, name autocomplete, red "overdue" after 3 days without payment, settle with one click.
  • Price list with old and new price — after a price rise the old price stays crossed out next to the new one, with a history of all changes.
  • Home budget — transactions in cents (no rounding errors), monthly limits per category with an "Exceeded" warning, season plan.
  • Backups with a checksum — one click, restore always via preview and confirmation; current data is automatically set aside before a restore.
  • Plain-language help everywhere — every page has an expandable "💡 Help — how to" in plain language, no technical jargon.
  • Windows app — the desktop window starts and stops its own local server; or you can run it with a double click in the browser.
