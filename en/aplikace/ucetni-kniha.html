What it does

Built because I got tired of switching between bank apps, broker websites, and Excel. I have 17 platforms where I keep money — XTB, Trading 212, eToro, Interactive Brokers, TastyTrade, Investown, Fingood, Wise, Revolut, crypto exchanges, two banks, pension, gold. To know my total I used to log in everywhere and add it up. Now I just open one page.

Invoices arrive on their own. A small home server (Home Assistant) pulls new invoices from Gmail every hour (utilities, telecom, insurance, payslips). The app suggests a category and I just click "post". No manual transcription.

Helps me plan retirement. Calculates expected state pension from real Czech Social Security data, how much I need to save myself, whether the current pace hits the 3.1M goal, and when exactly a position becomes tax-exempt under the Czech 3-year time test.

No cloud, no data leaving. Runs locally on Windows. Anything that syncs between PC and home server (Excel + invoices) is encrypted with military-grade AES-256 — even if someone got the file, they can't read it. GitHub is only encrypted transport, never plaintext.

Big rebuild (2026). Each part of the ledger now has its own section: Contracts and Insurance (loans, brokers, pension, car insurance), Payslips (gross/net salary, deductions, premiums, bank reconciliation), Overdue / Expected payments (matching by variable symbol — "overdue" is flagged only with evidence), Data Quality + Audit (which figures to trust), Charts + printable report, and Investments/Crypto as a register-only overview separate from regular spending. Email documents now flow through a smart "AI Documents" inbox: Gemini recognises them and suggests filing, I see a preview of the original and decide myself (confirm/edit/reject). Nothing is posted automatically — AI only suggests, I do the posting.

Key features

  • All money in one place — 17 broker platforms + 2 banks + crypto + pension + gold in one overview. One click, total visible.
  • Invoices from email automatic — Utilities, telecom, insurance, payslips — pulled from Gmail hourly. App suggests category, I just approve.
  • Retirement goal and plan — 3.1M CZK goal progress bar. 8-year projection of current pace. Pension calculator with real 2026 limits.
  • 2025 tax filing ready — Generates XML for the Czech tax office. Tracks 3-year time test — when shares become tax-exempt.
  • 26 pages for every detail — Cash Flow, Goal, Predictions, Investments, Crypto, Dividends, Tax, Pension, Knowledge Base, Lock-in counter — one page per question.
  • Encrypted, local, no cloud — No data leaves the machine. AES-256-CBC encryption. GitHub as encrypted transport only. Zero monthly fees.
  • AI Documents with confirmation — Gemini recognises a document (invoice, insurance, statement, payslip), suggests filing and shows a preview of the original. I decide with a click (confirm/edit/reject). Nothing posts itself; everything is audited.
  • Contracts, insurance, payslips — Dedicated overviews of liabilities (loans/brokers/pension), insurance (vehicle), and monthly salaries with deductions and premiums.
  • Overdue / expected payments — Payment matching by variable symbol; "overdue" is flagged only when evidenced, never guessed.
  • Data Quality + Audit + Charts — Overview of which figures to trust and what awaits review, charts of income/spending/cashflow and a printable report.
  • Invoices automatically from a home server — A Raspberry Pi (Home Assistant) pulls attachments from Gmail; the PC fetches them read-only into the inbox every hour, recognises them and offers them for approval.

📖 Dev diary — Finance — Ledger

  • v0.7.0 — big rebuild (June 2026) — ledger split into dedicated sections, phase by phase, always read-only and with no posting to the books without confirmation.
  • 2A — Contracts + Insurance — overviews of loans/brokers/pension and insurance (vehicle); sensitive numbers (accounts/IBAN/password) masked.
  • 2B — Expected + Overdue payments — data model, payment matching, the "Overdue" page; "overdue" only with evidence, otherwise "awaiting review".
  • 2B-VS — matching by variable symbol — precise pairing of a payment with a contract (without VS it is never marked paid).
  • 2C — Payslips — 59 monthly payslips (gross/net, deductions, premiums, allowances) + bank reconciliation.
  • 2D — Data Quality + Audit — overview of which figures to trust, what awaits review and what's missing, per area; pipeline audit log.
  • 2E — Charts + printable report — income/spending/cashflow/categories/salary charts + print via the browser; preview data clearly labelled.
  • 2F — Investments / Crypto detail — register-only overview (crypto, brokers, dividends, savings) separate from regular spending; no investment advice.
  • 2G — AI Documents: confirmation workflow — a proposal layer with decisions (confirm/edit/reject/ignore) and an audit trail; AI never writes by itself.
  • 2G — document preview — each proposal shows a preview of the original (PDF / image / masked text); protected PDFs flagged, everything local only.
  • Gmail → RPi → ledger (pull bridge) — a home Raspberry Pi pulls Gmail attachments, the PC fetches them read-only into the inbox (hourly task), the runner recognises them and offers them in "AI Documents".
  • v4/v5 (May 2026) — home ledger: banks, brokers, crypto, pension, invoices from Gmail, retirement plan. v5 extended with 6 crypto sheets.
  • Crypto refactor — "My crypto today" KPI, real fiat vs gross buys vs net, a filterable 1162-row table, NEEDS_REVIEW for the tax overview.
  • Security — everything encrypted (transcrypt AES-256), private GitHub repo. No cloud, no sign-up.
  • Status — working (not final), the 2025 tax pack awaits an advisor.
